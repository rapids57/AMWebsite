A band, half-eaten pizza pie, and a dream

4/16/2024

This sunday I went to a concert for a band called the Me First and the Gimme Gimmes.

Essentially, they're a cover band who do songs from different eras, such as 80s, 90s, and

surprisingly, even 2020s. Before we went to the show, we went to this eatery that served
